# Portfolio

This is a personal portfolio website for Adarsh Kumar, a Software Engineer based in India. It is designed to present his professional background, technical skills, service offerings, and selected projects in a polished and modern layout. The site works as both a digital resume and a visual showcase of his work for recruiters, clients, and collaborators.

## Overview

The portfolio is structured as a single-page experience that guides visitors through different parts of his professional profile:

- A landing section with a short introduction, profile image, and clear call-to-action buttons
- An About Me section that highlights his experience, education, and technical strengths
- A services section covering full stack development, frontend development, backend development, UI/UX design, and system design
- A work section that displays featured projects and links to GitHub repositories
- A contact section with a form and direct contact details
- A footer with social links and email information

## Experience

The portfolio highlights a range of projects that demonstrate his ability to build modern web applications and full stack solutions. - AI Short Video Ads Generator — a project that combines modern web development with AI-driven concepts. It highlights his interest in building innovative tools that can support content creation and automated workflows.
- Hospital Management System — a solution designed to support operational processes in a healthcare environment. This project demonstrates his ability to work with practical, real-world systems that require structure, reliability, and clear user workflows.
- Grocery Delivery App — a web-based application aimed at simplifying grocery ordering and delivery. It reflects his experience in creating user-friendly digital services that focus on convenience and efficiency.

Taken together, these projects show his capability to build applications that are both technically strong and useful in real-world scenarios. They also demonstrate his ability to work across different problem areas, from consumer-facing platforms to internal business systems.
